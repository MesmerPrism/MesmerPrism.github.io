Project boundary

Strobotorch Is Independent Android Tooling

Strobotorch is about torch and camera APIs, visual signal encoding, visual signal decoding, diagnostic capture, and safety documentation around bright flashing light.

Separate From Brain Candy

Strobotorch is separate from Till Holzapfel's scientific-advisor work for Brain Candy. Brain Candy is linked here only to make that distinction explicit.

Strobotorch is not part of Brain Candy, not a Brain Candy product, not sponsored by Brain Candy, and not a Brain Candy research deliverable.

What Strobotorch Is

Strobotorch is an open-source Android tooling project for public torch APIs, camera/luma diagnostics, torch event recording, visual signal encoding, visual signal decoding, and device-limit documentation.

What It Is Not

Strobotorch is not a state-shift system, hallucination tool, wellness device, medical device, meditation device, brainwave-entrainment product, or clinical intervention.
