Till Holzapfel

Mesmer Prism

 Mesmer Prism is Till Holzapfel's public interface for
 altered-perception design: XR systems, citizen-science tools, visual
 instruments, and essays that make breath, light, imagery, and body
 boundaries inspectable. The design philosophy is bounded transformation:
 let experience change, but keep agency, apparatus, variables, sources,
 and limits visible.

 Start with live systems, perception tools, embodiment essays, research maps,
 or publications.

Background

 My background moves between cognitive science, HCI, psychophysics, media
 theory, and research writing. The essays name concepts, lineages, and
 source limits before carrying those questions into XR systems, browser
 simulations, physiological sensing, and embodied prototypes.
